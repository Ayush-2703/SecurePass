Cybersecurity Tips

🚫

Never Reuse Passwords

Using the same password on multiple sites means one breach exposes all your accounts. Use a unique password for every service.

🔑

Use a Password Manager

Tools like Bitwarden, 1Password, or KeePass let you store all complex passwords securely behind one master password.

📱

Enable 2FA Everywhere

Two-factor authentication adds a critical second layer. Even if your password is stolen, attackers can't log in without the second factor.

📏

Length Over Complexity

A 20-character random password is far stronger than an 8-char one with symbols. Aim for 16+ characters on sensitive accounts.

⚠️

Watch for Phishing

Always verify the URL before entering your password. Legitimate services never request your credentials via email or SMS.

🔄

Check for Breaches

Visit haveibeenpwned.com regularly. If your account appears in a breach, change that password on every site where it was used immediately.
